## Legacy & Story

*"A candle may burn out — but the light of knowledge it kindles lives on."*

**"لﺎﺣ نﺎﺷ ،یﺿﺎﻣ نﺎﻣﺟرﺗ — A voice of the past, the pride of today."**

**Bab ul Ilm School** stands as a **living monument** to **Late Sir Muhammad Ramzan**, a man whose life defined the meaning of **true teaching**, **humility**, and **faith**.

He was more than a teacher — he was a **mentor of hearts**, a **beacon of discipline and compassion**, and a **devoted Muslim** whose every lesson began with "Bismillah" and ended with gratitude. For decades, he nurtured young minds in Mathematics, English, and the Quran — shaping generations not only through knowledge but through his **gentle character and spiritual warmth**.

During his life and after passing, his noble mission was carried forward by his beloved son, **Late Sir Muhammad Sarwar**, who continued this sacred work with the same sincerity and devotion for more than 20 years.

Today, this **torch of light** is now held high by another son of Sir Ramzan — **Hafiz Abdur Rehman**, the **brother of Muhammad Sarwar**, who has vowed to **revive, preserve, and expand this 50-year legacy of education and faith** under the banner of **Bab ul Ilm School**.

*"One life ignited a thousand minds — and their light still leads the way."*

This is not merely a school — it is a **continuation of a sacred journey** that began over half a century ago. It is the **living soul of a teacher's dream**, reborn to guide the next generations of students toward both **spiritual light** and **worldly excellence**.
